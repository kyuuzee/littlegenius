Before we start, let's watch a quick video to understand multiplication.


   Once you finish the video, let's try some simple questions.

Question 1


Zack has a stack of 4 coins. His dad gave him 2 more stacks of 4 coins. How many coins does he have now.


4 coins x 3 stacks = ?


Hint:


4    12    3    7

Question 2


Zack now has 3 stacks of 4 coins. His mum gave him another 3 more stacks of 4 coins. How many coins does he have now.


4 coins x 6 stacks = ?


12    6    24    4

Question 3


9 x 6 = ?


54    9    6    45

Hint: If you are having troubles just imagine the stack of coins.

Question 4


10 x 10 = ?


100    10    4    0

Question 5


12 x 12 = ?


12    2    14    144
