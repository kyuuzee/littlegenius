Question 1


Zack has 8 coins. He decided to evenly seperate them into a stack of 4 coins each. How many stack of coins does he have now.


8 coins ÷ 4 coins = ?


Hint:


4    2    3    7

Question 2


Zack has 16 coins. He wants to seperate the coins into 4 seperate stacks. How many coins does he have now.


16 coins ÷ 4 stacks = ?


12    6    24    4

Question 3


10 ÷ 5 = ?


5    2    6    4

Hint: If you are having troubles just imagine the numbers as the stack of coins.
