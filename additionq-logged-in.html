Question 1


Zack has 1 apple. His best friend, Mark gave him another apple. How many apple does he have now.


+ = ?


1    2    3    4

Question 2


Now Zack has 2 apples. His mum, Alice brought back 3 apples from the supermarket and gave it to him. How many apple does he have now.


+ = ?


5    2    8    7

Question 3


5 + 5 = ?


5    10    0    15

Hint: If you are having troubles just imagine the numbers as apples.

Question 4


14 + 13 = ?


27    17    7    2

Hint: To solve big number, break them down into smaller number first. Example: 12 can be broken down into 1 and 2 and 31 can be broken down to 3 and 1. Then add them according to their position. 12 + 31 will be 1 + 3 = 4 and 2 + 1 = 3. Then combine them to get the answer 43.
