Before we start, let's watch a quick video to understand subtraction.


   Once you finish the video, let's try some simple questions.

Question 1


Zack has 2 apple. He gave his best friend, Mark one of the apples. How many apple does he have now.


- = ?


1    2    0    4

Question 2


Zack has 5 apples. While studying, he eaten 3 of the apples. How many apple does he have now.


- = ?


5    2    8    7

Question 3


8 - 5 = ?


13    10    2    3

Hint: If you are having troubles just imagine the numbers as apples.

Question 4


25 - 13 = ?


12    17    7    2

Hint: To solve big number, break them down into smaller number first. Example: 34 can be broken down into 3 and 4 and 12 can be broken down to 1 and 2. Then minus them according to their position. 34 - 12 will be 3 - 1 = 2 and 4 - 2 = 2. Then combine them to get the answer 22.

Question 5


57 - 44 = ?


13    3    43    53
